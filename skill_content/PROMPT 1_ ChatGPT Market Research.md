## CRITICAL INSTRUCTIONS FOR CHATGPT

**TARGET OUTPUT:** 12+ pages of raw data

**DO NOT:**

- Write narrative paragraphs or summaries
- Synthesize data into flowing text
- Group quotes into themes in paragraph form

**DO:**

- List individual quotes one by one
- Include exact source attribution for every quote
- Count frequency of every theme
- Rank items by frequency
- Use plain text bullet points and lists only

**THIS IS DATA COLLECTION, NOT ANALYSIS.**

## PHASE 1: FOUNDATION SETTING

Establish clear definitions before data mining begins.

### 1.1 MARKET DEFINITION

Search: "\[product category\] market", "\[problem\] market size", "\[target demographic\] \[problem\]"

Define: Market category (e.g., "joint health supplements for seniors"), market size/scope (statistics), geographic focus (regions), source (where found).

### 1.2 PRODUCT NICHE DEFINITION

Search: "\[product type\] category", "types of \[product\]", "\[product\] vs \[alternative\]"

Define: Product category (e.g., "natural joint supplements"), product subcategory (e.g., "topical joint creams"), product format (pill/cream/powder), how this product type differs from alternatives, source.

### 1.3 SPECIFIC PROBLEM DEFINITION

Search: "\[problem\] medical term", "\[problem\] diagnosis", "what is \[problem\] called"

Define: Medical/clinical name (official term), common name used by market (what they call it), related conditions (connected diagnoses), frequency (how common - statistics), source.

### 1.4 GENERAL AVATAR

Search: "\[problem\] most common in", "\[problem\] demographics", "\[problem\] age group"

Define: Primary age range, primary gender (if applicable), geographic concentration (if available), general income level, common life situation (working/retired/caregiving), source.
